Common questions

I deleted the app and reinstalled it — where are my habits?

Your habits are stored only on your device, so removing the app removes the database. If your phone has iCloud Backup (iOS) or Google Drive backup (Android) enabled and you restore from a backup that includes the app, your habits will return with it. Otherwise they're gone, and we can't recover them — we never had a copy.

Reminders aren't firing.

First, check that EverMama has notification permission in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Notifications → EverMama; Android: Settings → Apps → EverMama → Notifications). If permission is on and reminders still don't fire, check that your phone isn't in a Focus / Do Not Disturb mode that silences this app.

How do I move my habits to a new phone?

Right now, EverMama doesn't have cloud sync — habits live on the phone they were created on. The simplest path today is to restore your new phone from a backup of the old one (iCloud or Google Drive); the app's database is included in those device-level backups. Cross-device sync is on the roadmap, and when it ships it will be opt-in.

Can you add a habit to the library?

Yes — please write to us. The library grows over time and we read every suggestion. Tell us what the habit is, when in the day it fits, and what you think it builds toward in the long run.
