Privacy policy

Last updated 2026-05-06

EverMama is built around a simple promise: your habits are yours, and they stay on your phone. This policy explains what that means in plain language, and what we do — and don't — do with your information.

What we collect

Nothing. EverMama does not require an account, does not ask for your name or email, and does not include analytics, ad SDKs, crash reporters, or third-party trackers. The app does not transmit your habits, completions, reflections, or reminder settings to any server we operate.

Where your data lives

Everything you create in EverMama — the habits you've added, the times you've completed them, your reflections, and your reminder schedule — is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database managed by the app. If you delete the app, that database is removed along with it.

Notifications

If you set a reminder for a habit, the reminder is scheduled by your phone's operating system (iOS or Android) using its local notifications system. The reminder content does not pass through any server. You can revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings.

Backups

Like other apps on your device, EverMama's local database may be included in the device-level backups your phone takes (iCloud Backup on iOS, Google Drive backup on Android), if you have those features enabled. Those backups are governed by Apple's and Google's privacy terms, not ours, and EverMama does not have access to them.

Children

EverMama is intended for adults — specifically, mothers caring for children. The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone of any age, since it collects no information at all.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add a feature that involves transmitting data off the device — for example, optional cloud sync between devices — we will update this page before that feature ships, and the app will ask your explicit, informed consent before any data leaves your phone. We will not make that change quietly.
